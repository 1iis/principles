# Slow then fast
> "[Make haste slowly][make-haste-slowly]" — Chandru Subramanian

The rhythm is simple: slow until the structure is clear; then fast.

The feeling of speed is not the same as actual progress. AI can create a flow-like state where code appears quickly, choices keep arriving, and the work feels alive, while leaving us with a system we do not understand, or with abstractions we did not choose, or with a pile of plausible pieces that do not quite fit. That is motion, not progress.

Rachel Thomas calls attention to the risk of “[junk flow][junk-flow]”: absorption that feels productive but does not make us or the project grow. We can map her observations to our literate source paradigm:

- Good *instructions* are much more than code. It's architecture, design, taste. Problem:

    > AI coding agents […] don’t produce useful layers of abstraction nor meaningful modularization. They don’t value conciseness or improving organization in a large code base.

- Good *explanations* are sharp, extremely relevant, and framed for maximum clarity. Problem:

    > AI can produce grammatically correct, plausible sounding text. However, it does not directly sharpen your ideas. It does not generate the most precise formulations or identify the heart of the matter.

- Good *flow* leaves us more capable. Junk flow leaves us with output we cannot maintain.

The slow part is where we produce understanding of the problem space: acquire signal, build the theory, find the boundaries, and decide what matters. It may take hours before the first function. That time is not waste if it produces a clearer pattern, a better interface, or a plainly simpler solution.

Once the foundation is solid, execution can become surprisingly fast and high in quality. The target is narrower. The examples are better. The model has less ambiguity to fill with guesses. We have more taste available at the point of selection.

Good examples can compress the slow phase because prior [understanding][understanding] has already been paid. The point is not to worship slowness. The point is to avoid confusing haste with leverage. [Research gives signal][research-as-signal]; [generation gives options][generated-buffet]; understanding decides what survives.
